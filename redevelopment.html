Redevelopment

/ redevelopment

Overview

Redevelopment transforms aging sites by demolishing old structures and constructing new, modern infrastructures, breathing new life into areas that have fallen into disrepair. Through this process, residents enjoy enhanced living spaces, state-of-the-art amenities, and larger homes, all at no cost, with additional benefits like improved safety and increased property value.

At Sanjay Developers, we approach redevelopment with deep respect for the character and history of each project. Our mission goes beyond simply replacing old buildings; we aim to rejuvenate them while honouring the legacy of the families who have lived there. By blending historical significance with contemporary design, we create residences that celebrate the past while embracing the future. We are dedicated to preserving cherished traditions while fostering new opportunities for community connection and growth.

Our Redeveloment Process

#1 Understanding Resident Needs

We begin by engaging with the residents to understand their expectations and specific requirements for redevelopment.

#2 Detailed Location Analysis

We begin by engaging with the residents to understand their expectations and specific requirements for redevelopment.

#3 Preparing the Redevelopment Plan

A detailed blueprint is created, showcasing proposed designs for indoor and outdoor spaces tailored to the residents’ needs.

#4 Aligning with PMC Regulations

The redevelopment plan is fine-tuned to meet Pune Municipal Corporation (PMC) guidelines, ensuring compliance with all regulations.

#5 Legal Agreement with the Society

Once the plan is finalized, a legal agreement is registered with the housing society, marking the official start of the project.

#6 Obtaining PMC Approvals

We proceed to secure all necessary approvals from PMC and relevant authorities for smooth execution of the redevelopment work.

#7 Relocation of Residents

Before commencing construction, residents are provided with suitable temporary accommodation to ensure their comfort.

#8 Redevelopment Execution

Demolition and construction begin as per the approved plan, with a focus on quality and timely delivery.

#9 Obtaining Occupancy Certificates

After completion, we secure occupancy and completion certificates from the authorities to ensure everything is in order.

#10 Handover and Welcome Ceremony

A grand handover ceremony marks the completion of the project, where we warmly welcome existing and new residents to their upgraded homes.

Our Project Specifications

Structure

  • RCC-framed structure designed to meet earthquake resistance norms, using only the highest quality materials for durability and safety.

Elevation

  • An elegant and impressive elevation that enhances the aesthetic appeal of the building.

Parking

  • Interlocking paving blocks with adequate lighting in the parking area, ensuring both functionality and safety.

Lobby

  • A grand entrance lobby featuring a smart card access control system for enhanced security and a welcoming atmosphere.

Digital Security

  • Each flat is equipped with a video door phone, and common areas are monitored with CCTV cameras to ensure continuous surveillance.

Firefighting

  • The latest firefighting equipment will be installed as per PMC guidelines, providing a high standard of safety.

Additional Security

  • All windows will be fitted with grills for added security, ensuring peace of mind for residents.

Sustainability

  • Solar water panels will be installed according to PMC guidelines, contributing to energy efficiency and sustainability.

Conservation

  • A rainwater harvesting plant will be implemented to conserve water as per PMC guidelines, promoting eco-friendly practices.

Compound Wall

  • Aesthetically designed and well-lit compound walls, with a beautifully crafted front compound wall façade for added appeal.
